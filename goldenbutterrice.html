Golden Butter Rice

Description

Golden Butter Rice is a simple yet delicious side dish that pairs perfectly with a wide variety of main courses. The combination of buttery flavor and fragrant spices makes it a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the rice to the saucepan and cook, stirring frequently, until the rice is lightly toasted, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Stir in the turmeric, garlic powder, and ground cumin until the rice is evenly coated with the spices.
  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Golden Butter Rice!
